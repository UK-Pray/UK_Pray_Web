What is Campus Pray?






Because You Prayed
God Touched our weary bodies with His Power.
And gave us strength for many a trying hour.
In which we might have faltered
Had not you, our intercessors,
Faithful been and true.

Because you prayed,
God touched our eager fingers with His skill,
Enabling us to do his blessed will.
With scalpel, structure, bandage-better still
He healed the sick and wounded, cured the ill.

Because you prayed,
God touched our lips with coals from alter fire.
Gave spirit fullness, and did not inspire
That when we spoke, sin blinded souls did see,
Sin's chains were broken - captives were made free.

Because you prayed,
Dwellers in the dark have found the Light,
The glad, good news has baniished heathen night.
The message of the cross so long delayed
Has brought them life at last because you prayed!


Selected, Revival God's Way
